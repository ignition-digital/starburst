Our Story

From a small family business to a nationwide fireworks distributor

Who We Are

Starburst Fireworks is a premier wholesale distributor of high-quality fireworks, serving retailers across Malaysia.

Our journey began with a simple passion for creating memorable experiences through the art of pyrotechnics. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of the nation's leading fireworks distributors, without ever losing our commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction.

Today, we work with manufacturers around the world to bring the most spectacular and innovative fireworks to professional retailers, event organizers, and fireworks enthusiasts.
